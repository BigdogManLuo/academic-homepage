## Research Interests

- **Optimization:** data-driven optimization, stochastic optimization, renewable energy trading
- **Forecasting:** decision-focused learning, probabilistic forecasting

## Engineering Skills

- **Data analysis & machine learning:** Time-series forecasting, end-to-end data pipeline, probabilistic forecasting, risk-aware optimization. (LightGBM, LASSO, Stacking...)

- **Optimization:** Hands-on experience with convex and mixed-integer optimization for energy systems, familiar with CVXPY, GUROBI/COPT Optimizer, PyTorch-based differentiable optimization.
